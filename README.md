
# ⚖️ Lawliet

**AI-powered legalization & legal assistance platform.**

---

## ✨ About

Lawliet is a modern platform that helps people and businesses with legalization processes, legal documents, and professional legal assistance.

The platform simplifies complicated legal procedures and makes legal services more accessible through technology and AI tools.

---

## 🚀 Services

- 📑 Document legalization
- 🌍 International legal support
- 🛂 Visa & immigration assistance
- ⚖️ Legal consultation
- 🖋️ Contract preparation
- 🔐 Secure document management

---

## 🎯 Mission

To make legal services simpler, faster, and accessible for everyone.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies

- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Node.js
- PostgreSQL
- OpenAI API
